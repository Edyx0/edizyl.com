# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential collaborators, employers, players, and fellow builders evaluating Ediz Yilmaz's independent apps, experiments, and product capabilities.

## Product Purpose

Present Ediz Yilmaz's portfolio as the work of a small, self-directed product studio. Success means visitors quickly understand the breadth of the work, remember its visual character, and can reach the relevant app, profile, or contact channel.

## Brand Personality

Arty-tech, impressive, smooth. The site should feel technically capable and visually authored without distracting from the products themselves.

## Anti-references

Generic portfolio templates, anonymous SaaS styling, and cinematic effects that become a gimmick or delay access to the actual work. In particular, avoid franchise-derived spectacle that overwhelms the site's own identity.

## Design Principles

- Make the work the proof: lead with shipped products and concrete experiments.
- Use motion to create flow, never to obstruct or postpone content.
- Keep the visual language distinctive enough to be remembered as Ediz's own.
- Balance artistic expression with fast, legible access to projects and contact details.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Preserve semantic structure, keyboard access, readable contrast, and reduced-motion behavior. No additional conformance target is currently required.
